# Energy Consumption Analysis 🌱

## Overview
An exploratory data analysis project examining energy consumption patterns 
across sectors to identify opportunities for sustainability improvements 
and energy savings.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Excel
- Tableau

## Key Questions Explored
- Which sectors consume the most energy?
- What time patterns exist in energy usage?
- Where are the biggest opportunities for reduction?
- How do renewable energy sources compare to fossil fuels?

## Analysis Performed
- Cleaned and processed energy consumption datasets
- Identified peak consumption periods and seasonal trends
- Segmented consumption by sector (residential, industrial, commercial)
- Compared renewable vs non-renewable energy usage patterns
- Built visualizations to communicate findings clearly

## Key Findings
- Industrial sector accounts for the highest share of consumption
- Peak usage periods align with winter months and morning/evening hours
- Renewable energy adoption shows 15-20% year-on-year growth trend
- Significant reduction opportunities identified in commercial sector
  through off-peak load shifting
